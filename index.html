Ashram

Programs Celebrated at Ashram

  • Kartak Sud 5 – Gnan panchmi- worship and pooja of books
  • Kartak Sud 14- Chaumasi Chaudash- Kshamapnana Pads and Bruhad Alochna
  • Kartak Sud 15 – Birthday of merciful God – Varghodo of God’s Chariot, worship of birthday celebration joyful pad
  • Chaitra sud 13- Birthday of Mahavir God – worship of joyful Bhavna Pads
  • Chaitra Vad 5 – death day of Mercyful God – worship of Virah Pad of merciful God
  • Vasishakh Sud 8 – Death day of Prabhji- Atma siddhi pooja and worship at night
  • Ashadh Sud 14 – Chaumasi Chaudas – pads of forgiveness and bruhad Alochna
  • Ashadh Sud 15 – Gurupurnima – Atma siddhi pooja and worship due to Devlali Temple Flag day
  • Shravan Sud 13 to Bhadrva Sud 5 – 8 days programme of Paryushan Parva- Samvatsari (on 8th day) Almasidhhi poojan, Samvatsarik Pratikrman
  • Aso Vad 1 – creation day of Altmasiddhishastra and birthday of Mercyful Prabhji – salute at all stories of Atmasiddhi
  • Aso Vad 13,14,Amas and Kartak Sud 1 – four day garland programme of meditation forever as order by Prabhji
  • Beyond above said parvas, each Purnima of every month Altmasiddhishastra pooja and pooja programme of Jinendra Panch Kalyanak on Ekam held regularly at temple.

Facilities Provided by the Ashram

  • Accommodation for up-to 55 people
  • 8 Rooms and 6 Self Contained Kutirs
  • Library rooms for study
  • Office and CD distribution room
  • Chitrashala depicting KrupaluDev’s JeevanCharitra

Rules of the Ashram

  • Stay up to Maximum 8 day in the Ashram.
  • It is essential to take part in every program of Temple.
  • Come in time for Worship and Swadyaya program
  • Follow brahmchrya.
  • Mobile use in temple premises is banned.
  • Come in time to kitchen for breakfast and dinner.
  • Those aspirants coming in to Ashram has to wear clothes in order.
